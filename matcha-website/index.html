About Matcha

Matcha is more than just a beverage—it's a journey into centuries of Japanese tradition. Made from shade-grown tea leaves ground into a fine powder, matcha represents the pinnacle of tea craftsmanship.

Each sip carries the wisdom of tea masters who have perfected their art over generations, offering you a moment of peace in our fast-paced world.

The Way of Tea

一

Prepare

Warm the bowl and whisk. Measure 2g of matcha.

二

Pour

三

Whisk

Create a gentle M motion until frothy.

四

Enjoy

Savor mindfully, appreciating each moment.
